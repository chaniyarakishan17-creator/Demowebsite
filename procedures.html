Expectations vs. Reality

Myth

"Surgery will make it huge instantly."

"Recovery is just laying in bed."

"The scar will be obvious."

Medical Fact

Surgery reveals hidden length (usually 2-4cm). True gains come from months of traction therapy.

Recovery requires active participation (hygiene, stretching, device use) to prevent retraction.

Incisions are placed in the pubic hair line, making them virtually invisible once healed.

Safety Protocols & Anesthesia

Anesthesia Management

We prioritize patient comfort and safety. Most lengthening and girth procedures are performed under **Twilight Sedation (TIVA)**.

Pre-Operative Assessment

Rigorous screening is mandatory. This includes a full coagulation profile, blood count, and ECG for patients over 40.
