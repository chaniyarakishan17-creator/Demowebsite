Frequently Asked Questions

Are the results permanent? +
Yes. The Ligamentolysis procedure provides permanent structural changes by releasing the suspensory ligament. However, maintaining these results requires mandatory adherence to the post-operative traction protocol for 3-6 months to prevent re-adhesion.
Does the procedure affect function or sensation? +
No. The surgery is strictly anatomical and does not interfere with the urethra, nerves, or erectile tissues. Sensation and erectile function remain unchanged. Our safety protocols are specifically designed to preserve full functionality.
How long do I need to stay in Berlin? +
We recommend a stay of 3-4 days. This allows for the pre-operative consultation, the surgery day, and a post-operative checkup to remove drains (if used) and ensure healing is progressing correctly before you fly home.
